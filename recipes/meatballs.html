How to cook: The Best Meatballs

Today we will cook The Best Meatballs

Detailes list of ingredients and recipe below :


Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Seasoning and herbs to your liking
  • Eggs to keep the meatballs from drying
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Italian bread
  • Water for moisture
  • Olive oil for frying

Steps

  1. Combine ground meat, your seasonings, Eggs and Cheese
  2. Slowly add the bread crumbs and water
  3. Shape into balls
  4. Fry the meatballs for about 20 minutes!
